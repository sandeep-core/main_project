The Power of BSF Farming

Nature's Ultimate Recyclers

At the heart of Waste to Wings is the incredible Black Soldier Fly (BSF). These remarkable insects are nature's most efficient decomposers. Their larvae have a voracious appetite for organic waste, capable of consuming twice their body weight every single day.

This natural process allows us to tackle the growing problem of food waste head-on. Instead of ending up in landfills, organic waste becomes the fuel for a new, sustainable resource. It's a perfect example of a circular economy in action, turning a problem into a valuable solution for our planet.
